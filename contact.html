Public Transportation Directions

Train

Take the R or M train to 36th Street. Walk toward 37th Avenue. Turn left at 37th Avenue. Turn right at 33rd Street. Office is on 33rd Street between 36th and 37th Avenues (Note: The M train does not run on weekends).

Take the N train to 36 Avenue Station. Walk toward 32nd Street. Turn right at 33rd Street. Office is on 33rd Street between 36th and 37th Avenues.

Driving Directions

Via Midtown Tunnel

After exiting the tunnel, stay to the right and take the 21st Street exit immediately after toll booth. (Note: unless you are in the far right lane you will not be able to take the 21st Street exit). Continue on this road as it goes under the highway until Jackson Avenue. Turn right onto Jackson Avenue. Stay on Jackson Avenue (Jackson Avenue becomes Northern Blvd. after the 59th Street Bridge overhead).Continue on Northern Blvd. for about four long blocks. Turn left onto 33rd Street. Office is on 33rd Street between 36th and 37th Avenues.

Via Triborough Bridge

After exiting toll booths, stay to the right and take the 31st Street exit off the bridge. Make a right onto 31st Street.Stay on 31st Street until 37th Avenue. Turn left onto 37th Avenue.Drive for two blocks then turn left onto 33rd Street. Office is on 33rd Street between 36th and 37th Avenues.

Via Queensboro Bridge (Lower Level)

As you exit off the bridge follow signs for Northern Blvd. Turn left onto Northern Blvd.Continue to 33rd Street and turn left. Office is on 33rd Street between 36th and 37th Avenues.

Via Queensboro Bridge (Upper Level)

Take the first exit on the right (21st Street) as you exit off the bridge. Make a right onto 21st Street. Continue until 37th Avenue. Make a right onto 37th Avenue. Continue until 33rd Street. Make a left onto 33rd Street. Office is on 33rd Street between 36th and 37th Avenues.

From Brooklyn and Staten Island

Take the B.Q.E. (Route 278) East to Northern Blvd. Make a left onto Northern Blvd. Turn right at 33rd Street.Office is on 33rd Street between 36th and 37th Avenues.

From the Bronx and Westchester via Whitestone Bridge

Whitestone Expressway (Route 678) to Grand Central Pkwy West.Take the Hoyt Avenue/31st Street exit (last exit before the Triborough Bridge).Continue going straight until 31st Street. Turn left onto 31st Street (under the elevated train). Proceed to 37th Avenue and turn left. Drive for two blocks and make a left onto 33rd Street. Turn left at 37th Avenue. Turn left onto 33rd Street.Office is on 33rd Street between 36th and 37th Avenues.

From Long Island via the Long Island Expressway

Take the L.I.E. (495) to Grand Central Pkwy West. Take Hoyt Avenue/31st Street exit (last exit before the Triborough Bridge).Continue going straight until 31st Street. Turn left onto 31st Street (under the elevated train). Proceed to 37th Avenue and turn left. Drive for two blocks and make a left onto 33rd Street. Turn left at 37th Avenue. Turn left onto 33rd Street.Office is on 33rd Street between 36th and 37th Avenues.
